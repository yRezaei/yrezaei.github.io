About Me

Who I Am

Hey, this is Yashar. I'm excited to share a little about myself with you. I consider myself quite an active person — I hit the gym three to four times a week, and I love playing volleyball. I'd even say I'm pretty good at it! Beyond sports, I spend my downtime gaming with friends. FIFA is our favorite, and we always have a great time competing against each other. I also enjoy camping during the summer, which gives me a chance to relax and connect with nature.

Now in my early 40s, living in Stuttgart, Germany, with my beautiful and beloved wife, Mozhgan. Life has been a journey full of surprises and opportunities, and I'm grateful for every step of it.

My Roots

I was born in Karaj, a city near Tehran, Iran. Over the years, Karaj has grown into a vibrant and independent state, but my family originally hails from Tabriz, a city in the mountainous northwest of Iran. Tabriz has a distinct culture and a history that I carry with me to this day. Growing up in Tabriz meant experiencing cold winters, snow-covered mountains, and a tight-knit community with a strong sense of heritage.

Iran itself is a country of contrasts. Each corner of the nation has its own unique culture, shaped by the languages, weather, and influences of neighboring countries. In the northwest, we speak a blend of Persian and Azerbaijani, while other parts of the country have their own linguistic and cultural mixes. This diversity is something I've always cherished, as it has taught me the importance of adaptability and understanding different perspectives.

Family & Values

Family has always been the cornerstone of my life. Losing my father at a young age was a profound and life-changing moment, but it strengthened my bond with my mother and my two sisters. My mother became the pillar of our family, dedicating herself to raising us with love, resilience, and unwavering support. She taught us to face challenges with courage and determination, and to always strive for excellence in whatever we do.

Despite the hardships, my family has always been a source of inspiration and pride. My sisters and I have grown into successful individuals, each following our unique paths while staying deeply connected. We owe so much of our success to our mother's sacrifices and strength, and I take every opportunity to express my gratitude for the values she instilled in us.

To this day, family remains a central part of who I am. The love and lessons I received growing up have shaped my approach to life, work, and relationships. I believe in the importance of loyalty, kindness, and supporting those around you — values that I hold dear in both my personal and professional life.

A Love for Animation and Games

I can talk about animations and games all day long — they're my absolute favorite topics. I'm always amazed by the details in the drawings and designs created by brilliant animators. For me, Hayao Miyazaki is the best of the best. His work with Studio Ghibli is phenomenal. My favorite character, it has to to be Totoro. Simple yet perfect in every way.

When it comes to games, I wouldn't call myself a hardcore gamer who spends endless hours playing, but I do enjoy following new releases and playing immersive games. My journey with gaming started with classics like Age of Empires, and over time, I became a huge fan of the Metal Gear Solid series by Hideo Kojima. More recently, I've been hooked on the Uncharted series.

What captivates me most about games is the storytelling. I love games that weave rich narratives, the kind that stay with you long after you've finished playing. That's why I'm drawn to animations and games with meaningful stories — they let us relive moments we've dreamed of or experienced in childhood. For me, it's the stories that make both games and animations so magical.
